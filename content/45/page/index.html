Frogger Remake

Frogger Clone Almost impossible for you to think of classic games and not remember Frogger , which despite its simplicity , both by the limitations of time as by today’s standards , also offers a mechanical fun and uncomplicated game, serving as a great pass time for some and a source of inspiration for others. This Frogger remake follows the line of the original, but with sound effects increase not originally available . The game is available open source for those who want to change it …. Developed in the Clickteam tool MULTIMEDIA FUSION 2.0. MULTIMEDIA FUSION 2.0 is an updated version of the first rapid development of tool kits , created by CLICKTEAM.COM the ” Klik and Play ” . The program is focused on the rapid development of games without the need to learn a programming language .
