Pac Man Remake

Pac Man Clone / Chomp Chomp Man Chomp Chomp Man – is a reissued version of the classic Pac Man, in one of his most famous conversions, the console Atari 2600. The game boils down to not just be an upgraded version , but has new features that do not exist in the original version . This game can be upgraded or modified at will, have fun . Developed in the Clickteam tool MULTIMEDIA FUSION 2.0. MULTIMEDIA FUSION 2.0 is an updated version of the first rapid development of tool kits , created by CLICKTEAM.COM the ” Klik and Play ” . The program is focused on the rapid development of games without the need to learn a programming language .
